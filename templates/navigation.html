Navigating through AMR

This page describes the various screens or pages you will come across while using the AMR app and how to navigate across them by using the menu.

Home Page

The app opens in the Home page, which lets you access other pages of interest from the hamburger menu (three lines) in the top left corner of the screen.

Search Page

Clicking on Search Manga in the menu takes you to the "search page" where you can search for a manga using the form. You will only get the top 20 results, so make sure you use some good key words.
The results of the search will be displayed in a new page. You can click on a manga title to view more details, or make a new search here.

Manga Home Page

Clicking on a manga title from the search results takes you to the "Manga Home" or the manga title page. Here you can see more details about the manga and scroll through the list of available chapters. From this page, using the menu option, you can add this manga to your favorites list.

Online Reader

Clicking on a chapter title from the Manga Home takes you to the "Online Reader". Here you can actually read the manga chapter. Note that clicking on the left side of the page takes you to the next page. Clicking on the right will take you to the previous page. The menu opens by clicking in the center of the screen. You can go back to the Manga Home, go to the next or previous chapter or select other options from the menu.

Favorites Page

From any page, clicking on "Favorites" in the menu takes you to the "Favorites" page, where you can see a list of the manga titles you have added. Clicking on these titles takes you to the "Manga Home" for that title. If you click on the "Remove" button, you can delete the title from your favorites.

Library Home Page

From any page, clicking on "Library" in the menu takes you to the "Library Home" page, where you can see a list of the manga titles whose chapters you have downloaded. You can manually add manga to your Library by making a new folder for your manga title under the "library" folder in ".AMR/static" after connecting your Kobo to your computer.

Library Manga Home

Clicking on a title from the library home takes you to the "Library Manga Home" for that title. Here, you can view a list of the chapters you have downloaded for this particular manga. Click on a chapter title to read that chapter in the "Offline Reader"

Offline Reader

The "Offline Reader" functions the same as the online reader described above. The only difference is in the menu options.

Shut down

From any page, you can click "Close app" from the menu to initiate app shut down. You should always do this before closing the browser. This stops the internal server from using CPU resources to serve pages on the localhost.

Navigating the app should be easy and intuitive once you know what each menu option does.
